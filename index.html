TL;DR: What We Offer

This page provides three simple tools for daily use: a Lotto Number Generator for random number picks, a Dinner Menu Recommender to help you decide what to eat, and an AI-powered Rock Paper Scissors game that recognizes your hand gestures.

How Can I Generate Lotto Numbers?

Feeling lucky today? Use our random number generator to create a set of lottery numbers. While we can't guarantee a win, we can guarantee a random and unbiased selection for your next ticket. Our algorithm is based on the robust Mersenne Twister pseudorandom number generator for statistically sound randomness.

What Should I Have for Dinner?

Stuck in a food rut? Can't decide what to eat? Let our Dinner Menu Recommender choose for you. From Sushi to Steak, let chance decide your next culinary adventure.

How Can I Play Rock Paper Scissors with AI?

Test your skills against our AI model or simply classify your hand gestures. This tool uses machine learning to recognize Rock, Paper, or Scissors gestures via your webcam or uploaded images.
